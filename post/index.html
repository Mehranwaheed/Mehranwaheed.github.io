Project II: Load Forecasting

Project Overview The Electric Load Forecasting project aimed to predict future electric load consumption, a critical aspect for efficient energy management. The project underwent thorough exploratory data analysis (EDA) to gain insights into the dataset, followed by various preprocessing steps to prepare the data for modeling. To optimize the performance of the predictive models, a grid search cross-validation approach was employed to fine-tune hyperparameters. Three distinct models, namely Random Forest, Decision Tree, and Feed Forward Neural Network, were evaluated.
